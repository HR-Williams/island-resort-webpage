Travel To Imaginary Island Resort

There are many ways to travel to Imaginary Island Resort. It's all about your budget and your schedule

  • Hot Air Balloon: If you're not in a hurry, and also don't mind if you don't actually arrive here, a hot air balloon ride can be the leisurely travel you didn't know you wanted
  • Catamaran: Also for the guest looking for an extended vacation where the journey is just as important as the destination
  • Teleportation: For those who just want to be on vacation already, we reccomend teleporation. You'll have the added adventure of inventing a teleporation device to make this a reality.
